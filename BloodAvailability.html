Blood Type Information

Understanding blood compatibility for donors and recipients

Type O

O- is the universal donor. People with O- blood can donate to anyone. O+ can donate to all positive blood types.

Type A

A+ can donate to A+ and AB+. A- can donate to A+, A-, AB+, and AB-. A+ can receive from A+, A-, O+, and O-.

Type B

B+ can donate to B+ and AB+. B- can donate to B+, B-, AB+, and AB-. B+ can receive from B+, B-, O+, and O-.

Type AB

AB+ is the universal recipient and can receive blood from all types. AB- can receive from A-, B-, AB-, and O-.
