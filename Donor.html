Donation Eligibility

Check if you're eligible to donate blood

You CAN Donate If

  • You are fit and healthy
  • You are between ages 18-70
  • You weigh more than 45kg
  • No major operations in last six months
  • May have high cholesterol
  • Taking blood pressure medication (if stable)

You CAN'T Donate If

  • You have a cold, sore throat or flu
  • You have a chronic infectious disease
  • You are taking antibiotics
  • You have had recent surgery
  • You are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Extended stay in certain countries
